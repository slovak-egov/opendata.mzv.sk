The portal opendata.mzv.sk was created with the aim of making available open data held by the Ministry of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic available to the public. The content of the portal consists of datasets in a machine-processable form according to the standards for providing data in an electronic environment in accordance with Decree No. 78/2020 on standards for information technology in public administration. The content of the portal and the terms of its use are subject to the Creative Commons CC0 licence.
