Ministry of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic is interested in ensuring the accessibility of its website in accordance with Act no. 95/2019 Coll. about information technologies in public administration and relevant implementing regulations to the extent of the conditions under Directive (EU) 2016/2102.

This accessibility statement applies to the website opendata.mzv.sk.

Compliance status

This website is fully compliant with Act no. 95/2019 Coll. on information technologies in public administration and relevant implementing regulations in the scope of conditions according to Directive (EU) 2016/2102.

Development of this Accessibility Statement

This statement was prepared on 29.09.2022.

Evaluation of website compliance with the requirements of Act no. 95/2019 Coll. on information technologies in public administration and relevant implementing regulations in the scope of conditions according to Directive (EU) 2016/2102 was carried out by self-assessment.

The statement was last checked 14.10.2022.

Feedback and contact information

In case of any problems with website accessibility, please contact us at the e-mail address: opendata@mzv.sk. The administrator and operator of the website is the Ministry of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic.

Ms. Kristína Spišáková of the Department of Digital and Press Agenda is the person responsible for accessibility and for the processing of requests sent as part of feedback.

Enforcement proceedings

n the event of unsatisfactory responses to the initiatives or requests sent as part of the public sector entity's feedback in accordance with  Art. 7 par. 1 letter b) directives of the European Parliament you can turn to the entity entrusted with the enforcement of the Directive, which is the Ministry of Investments, as part of enforcement proceedings, of Regional Development and Informatization of the Slovak Republic at the e-mail address: standard@mirri.gov.sk
