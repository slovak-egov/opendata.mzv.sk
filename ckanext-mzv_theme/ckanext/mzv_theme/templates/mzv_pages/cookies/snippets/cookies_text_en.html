To ensure the proper functioning of the website of the opendata.mzv.sk headquarters The Ministry of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic sometimes stores small data files on your device, so-called cookies.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit that page.

  • Host domain cookies are cookies stored by the website you are currently viewing. Only this page can read them.
  • Persistent cookies are cookies stored on your computer that are not automatically deleted when you close your browser, in contrast to temporary (session) cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.

How we use cookies?

The web pages of this website mainly use host domain cookies. They are cookies created and controlled exclusively by the Ministry of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic, not by any external organization.

How you can manage cookies?

Deleting cookies from your device

By clearing your browser's browsing history, you can delete any cookies that are already on your device. This will remove all cookies from all visited websites.

Please note, however, that you may also lose some saved information (for example, saved login data, favorite websites).

Managing cookies for a specific website

To get more detailed information about cookies for a specific website, check your privacy and cookie settings in your default browser.

Blocking cookies

You can set most modern browsers to prevent all cookies from being stored on your device. However, you may need to manually adjust some settings each time you visit the website. In addition, some services and functions may not work correctly at all (e.g. logging into the profile).
